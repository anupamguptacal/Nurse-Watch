About Nurse Watch

Nurse Watch is a wearable designed to help nurses in nursing home communicate to other nurses in case of an emergency as well as to set reminders for time sensitive tasks. This product is meant to make the lives of nurses who take care of multiple patients easier and also help them stay on top of their various responsibilities throughout the day.

About the Watch

The Nurse Watch has different components to help ease navigation and user interaction through the course of it's use. The Watch has a button clearly visible on the side of the external interface that brings the user back to the home page immediately irrespective of which page the user is in. In addition, the watch also features text to speech conversion to record different tasks as well as emergencies to reduce the amount of time required to complete tasks in these situations.
